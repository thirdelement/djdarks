About

Born in mid-70s south London, DRKS was raised listening to soul / rare groove and reggae. At a young age he was keen on vinyl on which he spent any spare pocket money.

When his cousin showed him the basics on a pair of Kenwood decks his natural skill and timing was clear. Since then DRKS has been influenced massively by jungle. In particular, the ambient, melodic tones played by LTJ Bukem, 4 Hero and liquid drum ‘n’ bass contemporaries.

In 2005 he met fellow DJ Clarky who introduced him to Virtual DJ which was a gamechanger. This sparked many hours of D‘n’B and House mix sessions together and developed DRKS into the accomplished artist he is today.

Whenever and wherever DRKS plays heads are nodding, the crowd is bouncing and the vibes are good.
