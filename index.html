About

Born and raised in mid-70s south London, DRKS was hooked by the club scene in 90s.

Inspired by his growing collection of reggae and hip hop vinyl, DRKS picked up DJing skills from friends and family.

Since these early days he has delighted clubbers, house party goers and home listeners alike with his well-constructed climatic sets.

DRKS' passion for drum 'n' bass and deep house will inspire and entertain you whereever you are.
